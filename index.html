Investment Objectives

1/ The Ethereum Vietnam Fund's primary investment objective is to provide investors with exposure to the Ethereum blockchain and generate income through staking, including capturing Miner Extractable Value (MEV) income. Staking income is generated through holding Ethereum in a validator node, enabling it to take part in the consensus process and validate transactions on the Ethereum network.

2/ Our approach is to minimize risk through a diversified strategy, utilizing our own proprietary staking system and also accessing various other liquid staking products available in the market. This approach allows us to minimize risk and maximize returns for our investors. Our team will continuously evaluate and adjust the fund's portfolio to ensure that it aligns with our clients' investment objectives and risk tolerance, while also keeping in mind the potential returns of staking income.

3/ By investing in the Ethereum Fund Vietnam, our clients will benefit from the long-term growth potential of Ethereum, while also receiving a stable income stream through staked income. Our fund's experienced professionals and focus on staking income will help provide strong returns to our clients and secure their investments.
